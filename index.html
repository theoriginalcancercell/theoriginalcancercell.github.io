What We Do

We are a company set on creating intriguing stories that will insite value in people's lives.

With the above goal in mind we had to choose a medium to express our stories in.

Why We Chose Games

We wanted to tell immersive stories in an interesting fashion

Games allowed us to tell players an interesting story that they can alter

When players play a game they get more story out of the lore than they would from traditional mediums

Also we find that making games is more of a challenge and who doesn't like a good challenge

We make games not because we play them, but because we want people to hear stories
